About Me

Hailing from Vermont, Mia has an eclectic background in both work and education. She began her journey studying animation at the Savannah College of Art and Design. She recieved her BFA in 2010 and has since included her art background in a variety of environments. Mia is currently coding, and strives to bring fun, creativity, and hard work into everything she does. Because of her deep love of winter and snowboarding, she now lives in Denver with her three cats where she draws fun pictures, creates embroidery, and codes on a regular basis.
